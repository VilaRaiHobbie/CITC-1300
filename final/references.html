In Sailor Moon

Sailor Moon has a lot of references in it. There are names, clothes, easter eggs of other animated works religious and historical references.

Names

  • Usagi Tsukino: Rabbit of the Moon
  • Ami Mizuno: Beauty of Water
  • Rei Hino: Spirit of Fire
  • Makoto Kino: Sincerity of Wood
  • Minako Aino: Beautiful Child of Love
