Naoko Takeuchi

Naoko Takeuchi was born on March 15th 1967. She earned a degree in chemistry but eventually found herself working on Code Name: Sailor V which led to Sailor Moon. She is married to the creator of YuYu Hakusho and Hunter X Hunter, Yoshihiro Togashi. Naoko made Sailor Moon based off of what she would have loved to have when she was young. Amazing friends. But she also wanted the story to have sex appeal, attractive characters, strong female lead. A lot of Naoko's interests can be seen in the anime and manga of Sailor Moon and once you see it and learn about it, it's hard to forget.
